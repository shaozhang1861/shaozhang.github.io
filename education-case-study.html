Case Study

AI As A Creative Partner

What I learned building a generative content system

What I've Done

Manually generated tags and recommendation texts limited both coverage and creativity on Meituan Homestay. I initiated a project to use LLMs for content generation and ML models for ranking relevance.

Coordinating six engineering teams and over 200 person-days, we built a system that rewrote thousands of listings dynamically.

Outcome: +0.29 pp conversion improvement, adding ¥320K GMV daily — but more importantly, hosts finally trusted automation because we kept human-editable options.

Reflections

AI doesn't replace creators — it augments them. Our biggest win was not the conversion uplift; it was transforming skeptical hosts into co-designers.

My Takeaways

Build opt-in loops. Give users control to review or edit AI outputs. Measure acceptance, not just accuracy. Human satisfaction is the real KPI. Treat models as teammates. Design feedback loops where users continuously train the system.
